Forgiveness is a virtue of Death Kwon Do. To eat The Death Sandwich you must first sign a waiver of death. You must have mullets and cutoffs. If you don't eat it right you die.

Ingredients

  • Foot long white sub bread
  • Ham

  • Sauce

  3. Karate chop bread in half

  5. Slam the meats, ham and meatballs, onto the sandwich
  6. Finish with the Death Kwon Do blessing
