The Broodwich

According to the "vouge" piece the broodwich is immortal. It cannot be killed or taken apart. As long as you don't ingest it you'll be ok. Are you the guy that told me to "beware"? Because I'll tell you where to be!

Ingredients

  • Bread forged in darkness from wheat harvested from Hell's Half-Acre and baked by Beelzebub
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • 666 seperate meats
  • dijon mustard
  • lettuce
  • sun dried tomatoes

Steps

  1. Have Beelzebub bake bread forged in darkness with wheat harvested from Hell's Half-Acre
  2. Slather with mayonnaise made from the evil eggs of a powerful dark chicken beaten into sauce by the hands of a one-eyed madman
  3. Add cheese boiled from the rancid teat of a fanged cow
  4. Stuff with 666 seperate meats
  5. Top and garnish with Dijon mustard and lettuce
  6. Bacon is extra
